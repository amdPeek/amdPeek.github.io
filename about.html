About me

I'm a computer science student. currently enrolled in a MSc at Paris Saclay University. My Major : Advanced networks & Optimization
Minor : Parallel & quantum Computing

I enjoy programming, focusing on portability issues, build systems, systems programming, multimedia and networking.

I started programming actively around 2019-2020 mainly in Java. I also have experience with languages such as Python, Ocaml, C/C++, php, Unix shell, assembly languages.

I have released several open source projects, some more complete than others, which you can find either here or on GitHub.

My operating system of choice is Linux (I often switch between Ubuntu,Debian or Kali distros), however, I try to make sure that my software works on Windows, Mac OS X and the various BSDs as well.

In 2022 I did an internship of 2 months in labs research of Paris Saclay. My main goal was to optimize relational databases.

I am very interested in cybersecurity topics. I have skills in this field due to available ctf's online. I train on platforms such as hackthebox and overthewire

Beyond programming stuff I really enjoy doing sports. I play tennis since I am 6 years old and I play in the handball university team.
